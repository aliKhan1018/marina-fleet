About Valquest

Launched by Bloemsma van Bremen in 2009 from the drawing boards of Dubois Naval Architects, VALQUEST is a high performance aluminium sailing yacht boasting a low profile deck house. Her elegant but simple interior design features high quality mahogany and a white leather finish throughout. She accommodates nine guests in four cabins, comprising of a large master suite, two doubles and one twin.
